Nyama Choma Recipe

Description

Nyama choma is basically grilled meat. Either beef or cow. Very Common in Africa

How to Cook Nyama Choma

Ingredients

  • Goat Meat
  • salty Water

Steps

  1. Put meat in an open fire over a grill
  2. Turn meat to ensure it is cooked on all sides
  3. Sprinkle salt water for flavour as you turn the meat
