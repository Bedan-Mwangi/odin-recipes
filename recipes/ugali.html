Ugali Recipe

Description

Ugali is a just a combination of maize flour and water. It is a staple food in East Africa.

How to make ugali

Ingredients

  • Maize Flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water over heat to a boiling point
  2. Add Flour Slowly until mixture is soft
  3. Use a mwiko to turn the ugali for heat to reach it evenly
  4. Leave it for 10-15min for it to cook well
